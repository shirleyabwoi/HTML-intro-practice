Day Two; Sunday

The second day had not so much going on. We were guided on rules that will be governing us. We did cleaning and got a chance to interact with the various parts of the school. We got to go to the classrooms and had a look at it. We were also handed laptops to be used for our classes.

Monday-Friday

At this point most of us were adopting to the new environment.Here is how the school's program runs on the weekday;

Breakfast by 6:20 A.M.

Cleaning our campus from 6;20 A.M - 7:00 A.M.

Attend class 7:00 A.M. - 12.00 P.M.

Lunchbreak 12:00 P.M. - 1:00 P.M.

Afternoon class 1;00 P.M. - 4:00 P.M.

Breaktime 4:00 P.M. - 5:00 P.M

Evening class 5:00 P.M - 8:00 P.M

Supper and eparting to the rooms from 8: 00 P.M.

Basically the weekday involved being guided on the operations of the school. We got various trainers who mainly did introduction of the classes they will be teaching us.
